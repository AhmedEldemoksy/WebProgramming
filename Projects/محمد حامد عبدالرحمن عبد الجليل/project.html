International career

Because of Mohamed Salah's brilliant level, he won many individual awards such as.
  • CAF African Footballer of the Year: 2017, 2018, 2019
  • PFA Players' Player of the Year: 2017–18
  • Premier League Golden Boot: 2017–18, 2018–19
  • FWA Footballer of the Year: 2017–18
  • CAF Team of the Year: 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019
Known as the "the Egyptian king " , Salah is a symbol of determination and success, inspiring millions of young Arabs.
