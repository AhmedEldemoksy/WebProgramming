About Mark

Mark Zuckerberg’s Achievements 1. Founded Facebook (2004): • Created Facebook while studying at Harvard, originally called TheFacebook. • It became the world’s largest social media platform. 2. Facebook Goes Public (2012): • Facebook was listed on the stock market (IPO), making Mark one of the youngest billionaires. 3. Acquisition of Instagram (2012): • Bought Instagram for $1 billion, expanding Facebook’s reach. 4. Acquisition of WhatsApp (2014): • Purchased WhatsApp for $19 billion, making it one of the biggest tech acquisitions. 5. Launch of Meta (2021): • Rebranded Facebook to Meta, focusing on virtual reality and the metaverse. 6. Expansion of Artificial Intelligence: • Invested in AI technology for Facebook and beyond. 7. Philanthropy: • Founded the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative with his wife to support education, healthcare, and science.
