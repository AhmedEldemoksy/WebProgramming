Mohamed Salah - The Egyptian King

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian footballer who plays as a forward for Liverpool and the Egypt national team. Known for his speed, dribbling, and finishing, he is considered one of the best players in the world.

He started his career at Al Mokawloon Al Arab before moving to Europe, playing for clubs like Basel, Chelsea, Fiorentina, Roma, and finally Liverpool, where he became a club legend.
