Professor Magdi Yacoub - A Legend in Heart Surgery Professor Magdi Yacoub is one of the greatest heart surgeons in the world, renowned for his groundbreaking achievements in cardiac surgery and organ transplantation. Born in Egypt in 1935, he became a global icon in his field, performing thousands of successful surgeries and pioneering advanced techniques to save lives. He founded the Chain of Hope Foundation and the Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation in Aswan, providing free treatment for children and adults in Egypt and the Arab world. Due to his dedication and humanitarian work, he has received numerous international awards and recognitions. 🫀 "Saving lives is my passion, and I dream of a world free from heart disease in children." – Magdi Yacoub

A journey of generosity that transformed millions of lives through innovation and humanity.
