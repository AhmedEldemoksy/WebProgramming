About Lionel Messi

The GOAT

Lionel Messi is one of the greatest football players in the history of the game. He was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. He started his football journey in Barcelona's famous academy and later became the star player for the club, leading them to many local and international championships.

Lionel Andres Messi grew up in the Al-Daai family and is one of four children. His father, Jorge Messi, was a steel mill manager, and his mother, Celia Cochettini, a part-time worker. Despite this, let's imagine them modestly, Messi's parents supported football, where Celia's renewed passion is particularly poignant.
